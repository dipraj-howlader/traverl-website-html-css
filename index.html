Eiffel Tower, Paris

The Eiffel Tower is an iconic Parisian landmark and one of the most visited attractions in the world. This wrought-iron tower stands at over 1,000 feet tall and offers stunning views of the city from its observation deck.

The Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is a massive fortification built across northern China over 2,000 years ago. This architectural wonder spans over 13,000 miles and is one of the most visited tourist attractions in the world.

Machu Picchu, Peru

Machu Picchu is an ancient Incan city located high in the Andes Mountains of Peru. This archaeological site is one of the most popular tourist destinations in South America, with visitors flocking to see its impressive stone buildings and breathtaking views.
